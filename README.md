## Passing Requirements

### To pass your road test, you must:

- **Daily Inspection Test:** Maximum 1 error allowed
- **Uncoupling & Coupling:** Up to 2 errors total, no more than 5 pull-ups
- **On-road & Backing Skills Test:** Up to 30 errors allowed, completed within 10 minutes
